About Us

Protecting the Livelihood of Canada's Independent Fish Harvesters

The Canadian Independent Fish Harvesters Federation (CIFHF) brings together fishing associations from British Columbia to Newfoundland, representing the collective voice of independent harvesters who depend on sustainable fisheries for their families and communities.

Our federation advocates at the national level for policies that protect the owner-operator principle, ensure fair access to marine resources, and support the coastal communities that have sustained Canada's fishing heritage for generations.

  • ⚖
    Policy Advocacy — Representing harvesters in federal fisheries policy, trade negotiations, and regulatory consultations.
  • ★
    Sustainability Leadership — Championing science-based management and responsible harvesting practices.
  • ✓
    Member Support — Connecting regional associations with shared resources, expertise, and a unified national platform.
